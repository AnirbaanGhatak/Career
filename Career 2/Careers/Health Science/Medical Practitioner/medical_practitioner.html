Medical Practitioner

Medical careers are available across a multitude of educational and skill levels, but also in diverse fields such as homeopathy or allopathy. After providing diagnoses, doctors treat patients who are suffering from diseases and injuries. Doctors are also called physicians and they can be either medical doctors (M.D.) or doctors of osteopathic medicine (D.O.).
